What we fix

Every door problem
we solve.

We specialise exclusively in door repairs across Byron Bay and the Northern Rivers. Salt air, humidity, and heavy use take a toll — we fix it all.

01

Sliding door repairs

Grinding, stiff, or completely stuck sliding doors. We replace rollers, repair tracks, and restore smooth operation on all aluminium, timber, and glass sliding doors.

Roller replacement Track repair Frame realignment
02

Screen door repairs

Torn flyscreen mesh, broken frames, damaged wheels, and faulty latches. We repair and replace sliding screen doors and supply matching mesh for all door colours.

Mesh replacement Frame repairs Wheel replacement
03

Lock & handle repairs

A faulty lock is a security risk. We replace sliding door locks, handles, latches, and deadbolts on all door types — same-week in most cases.

Lock replacement Handle repair Security upgrade
04

Bi-fold & stacker doors

Multi-panel bi-fold and stacker door systems require specialist knowledge. We service and repair all mechanisms, hinges, tracks, and locking systems.

Bi-fold systems Stacker doors Multi-panel
05

Commercial door repairs

Property managers and strata clients across Byron Bay — we service sliding doors in commercial properties, holiday rentals, and multi-unit residential buildings.

Strata properties Holiday rentals Commercial
06

Wardrobe sliding doors

Internal sliding wardrobe doors that jump the track, grind, or won't close. We repair bypass doors, mirrored panels, and wardrobe door rollers and tracks.

Bypass doors Mirrored panels Track repair
